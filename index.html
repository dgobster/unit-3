City of Madison Transit Demographics: 2010

Attribute Descriptions

Public transit accessability throughout the City of Madison's neighborhood planning districts, with associated area demographics.

Transit: Transit Stop Access (City of Madison). Percent of dwelling units that lie within one quarter mile walk of a regularly scheduled transit stop.
Homeowners: Owner Occupied Homes (Census 2010). Estimated percent of occupied housing units that are owner occupied.
White: White (Census 2010). Estimated percent of persons in the Non-Hispanic White alone race category.
Children: Families with Children (Census 2010). Estimated families with own children under 18 as a percent of all households.
Seniors: Senior Population - Age 65 and Over (Census 2010).

Data Sources:
